A Game Where You Play as a Bucket (AGWYPaaB) is a game being made in the Godot game engine.

It is currently being developed by E-DuzbNG, KAD, and Papyrus78677.

Other people who have contributed to the project are dogdillon, Whoeverhitme, DB, and NoahJam.

The full game doesn't currently have a planned release date, but the next update is planned to drop this year

AGWYPaaB will be available to play on Newgrounds.com, Windows, Linux, and Android.
