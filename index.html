FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q: What is AGWYPaaB?

A: A Game Where You Play as a Bucket (AGWYPaaB) is a game about a bucket and his friends going on silly little adventures. The game is made in GameMaker Studio with art made in Adobe Flash CS4

Q: Where can I play AGWYPaaB?

A: A Game Where You Play as a Bucket is currently ONLY available on Itch.io and our website. If you get it from anywhere else, it may not be safe. To play the official version, click the "Games" button at the top of the page.

Q: Why do you play as a bucket??

A: On September 17th, 2023, E-Dubz and KAD decided to draw some silly characters based on random objects. The first thing E-Dubz drew was a bucket with a smiley face. The rest is history.

Q: When is the full game coming out?

A: That question is hard to answer. We still don't fully know the scope of this project. Based on the stuff we want to add, it will probably be a few years from now.

Q: The prologue level has been out for so long, when will Bucket's story continue?

A: Soon... very very soon.
